About

Beara Seaview in West Cork is a 3 star Bed & Breakfast, approved by the Tourist Board of Ireland(Failte Ireland), a symbol of quality. All rooms are en-suite, with high-quality mattresses and bed linen. All have Flat screen TV, Wi-Fi and tea and coffee making facilities. Secure private parking. Channel View Bed & Breakfast caters for everyone, with Family, Triple, Twin, and Double Rooms available.


Beautifully situated in the picturesque village of Baltimore, West Cork with superb island and sea views. A family run Bed & Breakfast, where hostess Margaret Harrington is noted for her warm hospitality and where a warm Irish welcome awaits you. Located on an elevated site overlooking Baltimore Bay with mature landscaped gardens to relax and unwind.


Beara Seaview is an ideal place for touring West Cork and Kerry.

Directions

Take the N71 to Skibbereen and continue on the R595 to Baltimore. Our Baltimore Guest Accommodation at Channel View is on the left hand side of the road 0.5km before Baltimore Village.


Driving West, go through Castletownbere, past O’Regan’s Garage, and look for the B&B sign 100 meters on the left.


Directions from Castletownbere to O’Sullivan’s Summerhill Guesthouse: Just a 7 minute walk from the town centre along the Allihies road.
